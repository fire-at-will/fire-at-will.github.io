About Me

Hi! I'm Will Taylor and I'm a student in Texas Christian University's class of 2017. I'm originally from Jackson, Mississippi, and I'm having a blast in my new home in Texas. I'm currently pursuing a BS in computer science with a minor in general business.

While at TCU, I spend my time outside the classroom playing my trumpet for the TCU Marching Band, participating in many academic clubs, and exploring the great state of Texas. I've spent my summers working jobs related to my degree. During the summer of 2014 I worked as an Android developer for TCU, and in 2015 I worked as an intern for C Spire. These jobs really helped me understand the business side of what people in computer science related fields do every day.

After graduating from TCU, I plan to get a job in software engineering in a tech company or department.
